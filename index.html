    FAQ

    COMMON QUESTIONS

    Is the free tier actually free — no hidden costs? +
    Yes. The free tier is completely free. All 10 modules, 75+ lessons, the RPBG indicator, the IB LVN Rejection strategy, and the pre-market checklist — free. No credit card required to join. The paid tier is a separate upgrade for members who want live sessions, full playbook access, and direct coaching.
    I've never traded before. Is this right for me? +
    Yes — Module 0: The Starting Line was built specifically for complete beginners. It assumes zero prior knowledge. You'll cover financial markets, how to read a chart, candlesticks, basic terminology, and whether trading is even right for you — before a single futures lesson begins.
    Will this curriculum make me profitable? +
    Honest answer: no curriculum can guarantee profitability. This one will make you knowledgeable — you'll understand the instruments, the structure, the framework, and have a real executable strategy. Profitability requires screen time, live experience, and the emotional reality of real capital at risk. That gap is what the paid tier and live coaching are designed to close.
    What instruments does this cover? +
    The curriculum focuses on ES (S&P 500 futures), NQ (Nasdaq futures), and 6E (Euro/USD futures). These are the three instruments in the TarrierFuture playbook. The methodology and frameworks taught are applicable to other futures instruments, but the examples and setups are built specifically around ES, NQ, and 6E.
    Do I need MenthorQ or Tradytics to follow the curriculum? +
    No — not for the free curriculum. Module 5 teaches you how these tools work and what they provide, but you can follow all free content with just a TradingView account (free tier). MenthorQ and Tradytics become more relevant in the paid tier where real-time data is part of the live session workflow.
    What is prop trading and do I need it? +
    Prop trading (proprietary trading) allows you to trade a funded account provided by a firm — meaning you can trade significant capital without risking your own. Module 9 covers prop trading in full. It's not a requirement to benefit from this curriculum, but it is the path I personally use and recommend for traders who want to scale without large personal capital.
    When will the paid tier launch? +
    The paid tier is in development alongside the free curriculum. Join the free community now to be first notified when it launches — members who join early will receive a founding member rate. The free curriculum will always remain free.
